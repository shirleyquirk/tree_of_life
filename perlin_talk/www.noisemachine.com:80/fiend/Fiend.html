THE POLITICIAN

From out the Pit, the one shall rise
To lead revolt amongst the Fallen.
As it is written, so shall it be:
``The war for Heaven is fought on Earth...''

               

EXPLANATION

Subject: I was interested in how a fiend would gesture if he were giving a political speech. So I did this study. You could say that he's my best fiend.

Tech notes: This is a pure Java 1.0 applet, using my own modeler and real-time renderer. Body movement is all sine functions.

Instructions: Drag with your mouse to change the camera view. Depending on your computer speed, you may want to try it smaller or larger.
